2. The service

AnalyzeMyCar provides vehicle information reports for cars in the UK. A report can include a vehicle overview and condition grade, MOT and mileage history, a digital service book, a factory build sheet, a grade-based market valuation, and an optional AI visual condition assessment. Reports are built from third-party data sources including DVLA, DVSA and OEM data.

5. AI Visual Assessment

The AI Visual Assessment estimates a vehicle's condition and likely repair costs from photographs using automated analysis. It is an estimate, not a physical inspection, and it depends on the quality and accuracy of the photos provided. Figures are indicative and should not be relied on as a substitute for a professional inspection.
