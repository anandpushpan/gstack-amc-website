# v1.6 Changes: Design-review fixes (section rhythm + type scale)

**Date**: 2026-06-13
**Source**: gstack /design-review pass on v1.5 (Playwright render + DOM measurement at 1280px and 390px)

## Why this version exists

A design review of the v1.4/v1.5 additions found the new sections were strong
(real screenshots, no AI-slop patterns, clean mobile reflow) but two issues
remained. Both are fixed here.

## What changed

### 1. Restored background alternation (the headline fix)
The page separates sections by alternating white and soft-grey backgrounds. The
v1.4 deep-dive sections had landed white, next to existing white sections,
creating two white-on-white seams where the section boundary visually
disappeared:
- features (white) + "Everything the competition is missing" (white)
- "Service and build records" (white) + dealers band (white)

Fix: flipped three section backgrounds so the whole page alternates perfectly
again (W S W S W S W S W S W S W, zero collisions):
- "Everything the competition is missing" → soft
- "Service and build records that prove the story" → soft
- "From reg to real value in three steps" (how-it-works) → white

CSS-class change only, fully reversible. Verified by DOM: no two adjacent
sections share a background.

### 2. Tightened the H3 type scale
H3 was rendering at five sizes (15/16/17/18/20px). The v1.5 module cards used a
one-off 16px. Bumped module-card h3 to 17px to match the feature cards, reducing
the scale to four distinct H3 sizes.

## Known, not fixed (pre-existing, minor)
- The hero lookup card overhangs ~4px at 390px (plate input width). Pre-existing,
  not introduced by the new sections. Deferred as polish.

## Verification
- Section sequence: W S W S W S W S W S W S W (0 collisions, measured).
- H3 sizes: 15/17/18/20px (4 distinct, down from 5).
- Re-rendered full page at 1280px; alternation reads cleanly at every boundary.
- 0 em/en dashes.
