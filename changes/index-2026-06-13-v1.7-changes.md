# v1.7 Changes: Module picker moved into the hero (Option A)

**Date**: 2026-06-13

## Why this version exists

The live product's most engaging moment is choosing which modules go into your
free report, right at the top, next to the reg/VIN input. Our draft only showed
that choice as an explanation buried at section 9. So a visitor met a vague
"first report FREE" promise and had to scroll most of the page to learn what
that meant or that they got to choose. This version brings the choice into the
hero so it becomes the hook, matching the real product.

## What changed

### 1. Interactive module picker in the hero lookup card
Below the reg/VIN input, the four modules now appear as tappable rows:
- Basic Info, Build Sheet + Valuation, DSB + MOT: pre-ticked, green, "Free"
- AI Visual Assessment: unticked, "+4 cr" badge (purple, the paid-add-on colour)

A live summary line updates as you toggle:
- Default: "Your first report: free, 3 modules included"
- With AI Visual on: "...free, 3 modules included + AI Visual, 4 credits" (the
  paid part in purple, so it's honest that AI Visual costs credits even on the
  first report)
- All unticked: "Pick at least one module to run your report."

This makes "free" tangible (three ticked modules you can see) instead of a vague
claim, and mirrors the exact interaction users meet in the product.

### 2. Hero hint updated
"Enter a reg or VIN, then pick what goes in your report below. Your first one is
free, no card needed."

### 3. Deeper module section reframed as the "more cars" credit story
The section that used to first-reveal the modules ("Four modules. Pick the ones
you need.") now leads with "After your free report / Checking more cars? Here's
how credits work." It reinforces the hero choice and explains the credit model
for subsequent cars, instead of duplicating the first reveal. The four module
cards and the "how to spend credits efficiently" guide are unchanged.

## Accessibility
- Picker rows are real `<button>` elements with `aria-pressed` reflecting state.
- Keyboard focusable; the existing focus-visible ring applies.

## Verification
- Toggle logic verified across all states (default, AI on, partial, empty).
- Rendered at 1280px and 390px; picker reflows full-width on mobile.
- No horizontal overflow at 390px (document width == viewport, the prior 4px
  hero overhang no longer produces page scroll).
- 0 em/en dashes.

## Credit model (unchanged, owner-confirmed)
Basic Info 1cr, Build Sheet + Valuation 2cr, DSB + MOT 3cr, AI Visual 4cr; full
report = 10cr. First report free for everything EXCEPT AI Visual, which is a paid
add-on even on the first report.
