compared them against our v2.1 drafts. The comparison closed most of our open
`[TO CONFIRM]` items and surfaced three factual conflicts plus the
finance-database question. The owner made the calls; this version applies them.

## Decisions applied

1. **Credit expiry: keep 180 days.** Our homepage and legal pages keep "credits
   expire 180 days after purchase." The live Terms currently say credits do not
   expire while the account is active; those are out of date on this point and
   should be updated to match us.
2. **Finance databases: keep omitting.** We do NOT claim finance / write-off /
   stolen data. Our Terms explicitly state we do not provide those checks. (The
   live Terms claim "finance databases" as a source; we are not copying that
   until the product is confirmed to deliver it. Honesty guardrail.)
3. **Contact + refund timing: match the live operative text.** Legal pages now
   use support@analyzemycar.com and "around 14 business days" for refund
   processing.
