## What changed

### 1. Hero optimised for mobile
- Dedicated phone tuning at `<=560px`: smaller display headline (29px), tighter spacing,
  trimmed badge/sub/trust-row sizes, smaller plate input and GB tab so nothing overflows, and the
  forced `<br>` in the headline is hidden so the text wraps naturally.
- **Bug found and fixed during QA:** hiding the `<br>` had glued "is" and "really worth" together
  ("isreally worth"). Added a space in the markup so it reads correctly on every width.
- Verified with real screenshots at 390px and 1280px.

### 2. Real report screenshots replace the CSS mockup
- The "A look inside your report" section now shows **real screenshots from the sample report**
  (`Sample_AnalyzeMyCar_Report_2026-05-22.pdf`), not a hand-built mockup:
  - `assets/report/report-valuation.jpg` — the valuation page (market value, refurb cost, final
    value), the screenshot that ties directly to the moat message. Shown as the featured image.
  - `assets/report/report-overview.jpg` — vehicle overview with condition grade and spec.
  - `assets/report/report-trend.jpg` — the valuation-trend chart by grade.
- Pages were rendered at 130 DPI and cropped to their content region. Images use `loading="lazy"`
  and explicit width/height to avoid layout shift.
- Removed the now-unused `.doc*` CSS mockup; added a small `.report-gallery` (featured image +
  two thumbnails, stacks on mobile).

## QA method
Rendered the live file in headless Chromium (Playwright) at mobile (390px) and desktop (1280px),
screenshotted the hero and the report-preview section, and corrected what the screenshots revealed.
