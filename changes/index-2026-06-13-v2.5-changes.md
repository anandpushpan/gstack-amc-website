# v2.5 Changes: Mobile header, alignment consistency, sticky-CTA timing

**Date**: 2026-06-13

## What changed (mobile only; desktop unchanged)

1. **Removed the top "Check a car" CTA on mobile.** The header now shows just the
   logo and the menu button. The action-first hero and the sticky bottom bar
   already cover the call-to-action, so the top button was redundant.

2. **Consistent section-header alignment.** All section headers (overline + h2 +
   lead) are now centred on mobile. Three overlines (moat, report preview, dealer)
   had an inline `text-align:left` that left them misaligned against their centred
   headings; they now centre on mobile. Body content (sub-headings, paragraphs,
   bullet lists) stays left-aligned, as before.

3. **Sticky bottom CTA now appears only after the hero.** The bar was triggered
   with a 60px early margin; it now appears precisely once the hero section has
   fully scrolled out of view (IntersectionObserver threshold 0, no root margin).
   Verified: hidden at the top and throughout the 1058px hero, appears once past it.

## Verification
- Header `.btn-grad` display:none on mobile.
- All six section overlines compute to text-align center on mobile.
- Sticky bar: hidden at top, hidden while any part of the hero is visible, shown past it.
- Desktop unchanged. 0 em/en dashes.
