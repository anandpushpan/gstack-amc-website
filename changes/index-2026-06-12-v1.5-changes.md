# v1.5 Changes: Credit pricing corrected to match the live product

**Date**: 2026-06-12

## Why this version exists

A fact-check against the live site (analyzemycar.com) found the draft's credit
pricing was wrong in four places. The live product uses a **module picker**:
users tick which modules go into their report. The real modules and prices:

| Module | Credits | In free first report? |
|--------|---------|----------------------|
| Basic Info | 1 | Yes |
| Build Sheet + Valuation (bundle) | 2 | Yes |
| DSB + MOT (bundle) | 3 | Yes |
| AI Visual Assessment | 4 | No, paid add-on (owner-confirmed) |
| Full report (all four) | 10 | n/a |

The draft previously priced Build Sheet, Valuation, DSB and MOT as four
separate modules (summing to 15) and invented three bundles that don't exist
(Quick 3cr / Full 10cr / Premium 14cr).

## What changed

1. **"Flexible credit system" section replaced** with a module-picker replica:
   four cards matching the live product's checkboxes (names, bundles, credit
   prices), each marked "Free in your first report" except the AI Visual
   Assessment (marked paid add-on).
2. **"How to spend credits efficiently" guide added** below the picker:
   three worked scenarios (shortlist at 3cr/car, viewing at 6cr, negotiation
   at 10cr all-in) so buyers understand why they're buying credits and how to
   stretch them.
3. **FAQ "How do credits work?" corrected** in both the visible FAQ and the
   JSON-LD structured data (the old wrong prices were being published to
   search engines).
4. **All six feature-card badges relabeled** with their real bundle names
   (e.g. "DSB + MOT · 3 credits" instead of a standalone "3 credits").
5. **Hero lookup hint updated**: "You choose what goes in your report; the
   first one is free" — the free promise is now concrete and mirrors the
   real product flow users will meet after the CTA.
6. **One stray en dash removed** from a v1.4 figcaption ("graded A to C").

## Verification

- Screenshot-verified at 1280px and 390px (4-col grid stacks to 2-col then 1-col).
- 0 em/en dashes (grep-verified).
- 0 references to the removed fake tiers.
- Credit math consistent everywhere: 1 + 2 + 3 + 4 = 10 = full report.

Live-site screenshots provided by the owner (module picker with hover
