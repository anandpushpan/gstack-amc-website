# v2.1 Changes: Legal pages (Privacy, Terms, Refund) — drafts

**Date**: 2026-06-13

## Why this version exists

The site's footer linked to Privacy, Terms and Refund pages that didn't exist
(dead `#` links). A paid product that takes card payments can't launch without
them. v2.1 adds all three as drafts and wires the footer links.

## What changed

### New pages
- **privacy.html** — UK GDPR-structured privacy policy: who we are, what we
  collect, legal bases, the vehicle data we look up, sharing, retention,
  international transfers, your rights, cookies, security.
- **terms.html** — service description, account terms, the credit model
  (1/2/3/4 modules, full report 10, 180-day expiry), AI-estimate disclaimer,
  accuracy disclaimer (reports are guidance, not a warranty; no
  finance/write-off/stolen claims), acceptable use, IP, liability, governing law
  (England and Wales).
- **refund.html** — grounded in the homepage FAQ terms: unused credits
  refundable within 14 days, used credits charged at GBP 1 each, VAT
  non-refundable, refunds in 5 to 10 business days, expired credits not
  refundable. Includes a worked example and a statutory-rights note.
- **assets/legal.css** — shared stylesheet for the three pages (design tokens,
  header/footer, legal prose, draft banner), mobile-first.

### Homepage
- Footer Privacy / Terms / Refund links now point to the new pages (were `#`).

## Important: these are DRAFTS

Every legal page carries a visible amber "Draft, pending legal review" banner.
All clauses are grounded in confirmed facts (company details, the credit model,
the refund terms). Genuine unknowns are marked inline as `[TO CONFIRM]` rather
than invented. Before launch these MUST be reviewed by a qualified
adviser. Open items flagged in the drafts:
- Privacy: ICO registration number, payment processor name, sub-processors /
  hosting providers, data retention periods, cookie list, transfer safeguards.
- Terms: liability cap and exclusions, dispute resolution.
- Refund: exact statutory cancellation wording for digital content.
