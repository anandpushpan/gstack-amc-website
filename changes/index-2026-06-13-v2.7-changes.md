# v2.7 Changes: Fix module-card credit chip overflowing on mobile

**Date**: 2026-06-13

## The bug

After the v2.6 card redesign, the credit chip sat on the same row as the icon
with `justify-content:space-between` and `white-space:nowrap`. On phones the
longer labels ("DSB + MOT · 3 credits", "Build Sheet + Valuation · 2 credits")
were wider than the space left beside the icon, so the chip ran past the card's
right edge and got clipped at the screen edge.

## The fix (mobile only, <=560px)

- `.feat-top` now stacks as a column on mobile: the icon sits on its own line and
  the credit chip drops to the line below, left-aligned. Consistent across all
  six cards, so no chip is ever squeezed beside the icon.
- The chip font is trimmed to 10.5px on mobile with `max-width:100%` as a guard.

## Verification

- Chip overflow measured at 320px, 360px and 390px: 0px on every card.
- Longest label ("Build Sheet + Valuation · 2 credits") fully visible.
- Desktop unchanged (icon + chip stay on one row, 3-up grid). 0 em/en dashes.
