Refund Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026 · Version 1.1 (draft)
⚠ Draft, pending final legal review. Reconciled with our published terms. Figures are confirmed; the statutory-rights wording should still be checked by an adviser before launch. Not legal advice.

This policy explains when and how you can get a refund on credits you have bought from AnalyzeMyCar.

Your first report is free

Your first report is free and includes every module except the AI Visual Assessment. There is no charge for it, so there is nothing to refund.

Refunds on credits

  • You may request a refund within 14 days of the date you bought the credits.
  • Refunds are calculated on unused credits only. A credit counts as used the moment a report is successfully generated and made available in your account, whether or not you access, download or use it.
  • Each used credit is charged at £1.00 and is non-refundable.
  • VAT is non-refundable.
  • Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method within around 14 business days.
  • Credits expire 180 days after purchase. Expired credits cannot be refunded.

A worked example

You buy 20 credits and use 6, then request a refund within 14 days. We refund the 14 unused credits (including the applicable VAT on those credits). The 6 used credits are charged at £1.00 each and are not refunded.

Your statutory rights

If you are a consumer, you may have a legal right to cancel within 14 days under the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013. Because credits give immediate access to digital content, you agree that the service begins immediately and your right to cancel may be lost once a report has been generated and made available in your account. This policy does not remove any statutory right you have. The precise statutory wording should be confirmed by an adviser before launch.

How to request a refund

Email support@analyzemycar.com from the address on your account, with the subject line "Refund Request". Include the transaction ID from your receipt and the number of unused credits you want refunded. We will acknowledge your request and confirm the refund amount before processing it.

Questions

For anything about refunds or billing, contact support@analyzemycar.com.
