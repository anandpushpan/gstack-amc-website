2. What we collect and why

Account information

Your name and email address when you create an account. If you sign in with Google, we receive basic profile information (name, email) from Google. Used to create your account, authenticate you and send service messages.

Vehicle data

The registration (VRM) or VIN you enter, the report data we retrieve about that vehicle, and any photographs you upload for a Visual Assessment. Used to generate and deliver your reports. We may use anonymised or aggregated data to improve our systems. Photos are not used for facial recognition or biometric identification.

Payment information

Only limited transaction data (confirmation, amount, email). Your full card details are never collected or stored by us; payment is handled entirely by our third-party payment processor [TO CONFIRM].

Usage data

IP address, browser type and pages visited, for security, performance and improving the service. Analytics are used only with your cookie consent.

3. Our data sources

When you check a vehicle, we retrieve information from official and industry-recognised third-party providers, including DVLA vehicle data, DVSA MOT records, and OEM build and service data through the AutoDAP network. This data describes the vehicle, not you. It is provided to us "as is" and reflects the source database at the time of retrieval; we do not independently verify it, so please verify before relying on it. You are responsible for having a legitimate reason to look up a vehicle you do not own.

5. Who we share data with

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with:

  • Vehicle data providers we query (DVLA, DVSA, OEM via AutoDAP), who may act as independent controllers for that data.
  • Our payment processor, to take payment [TO CONFIRM].
  • Our sign-in provider (Google), which receives basic profile information if you choose Google Sign-in.
  • Analytics providers, receiving anonymised or aggregated data, with your consent.
  • Hosting and email providers acting as our data processors under formal agreements [TO CONFIRM: named providers].
  • Legal or regulatory authorities, where we are required by law.

6. International transfers

Because AnalyzeMyCar is powered by a Netherlands-based group and some providers operate globally, some data may be processed outside the UK. Transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards under applicable data protection law (for example, the UK adequacy decision for the EEA or the UK International Data Transfer Agreement). For details, contact support@analyzemycar.com.

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access your data, correct it, ask us to erase it, restrict or object to processing, and request portability. Where we rely on consent, you can withdraw it at any time (this does not affect processing already carried out lawfully). To exercise any right, email support@analyzemycar.com; we respond within one calendar month. You may also complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

9. Cookies

We show a cookie banner on your first visit. Essential cookies run the site and keep you signed in and need no consent. Analytics and marketing cookies are set only with your consent, which you can change or withdraw at any time in cookie settings. A full list of cookies, their purpose and lifespan: [TO CONFIRM].

10. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including encryption in transit, secure credential storage and access controls. Payment data is processed entirely by our third-party processor and never stored on our servers. No method is completely secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your data and will notify you and the ICO of a breach where the law requires.

11. Automated decision-making

We use AI to analyse vehicle data and generate reports, including the Visual Assessment, which estimates likely repair costs from photos. These outputs are indicative only and may contain inaccuracies, omissions or approximations. They are not used to make decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects about you, and you should verify them independently.

12. Children's privacy

AnalyzeMyCar is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data about minors and will promptly delete it if we discover it. Report any concern to support@analyzemycar.com.

13. Marketing and social media

With your consent we may send promotional emails and notifications. You can opt out any time via the unsubscribe link or by emailing us; this does not affect prior lawful processing. We maintain social media profiles (for example Facebook and LinkedIn); your interaction there is also governed by those platforms' own terms. Please do not share sensitive personal information on public pages.

14. Changes and contact

We may update this policy as the product changes; we will post the new version here with a revised date, and give additional notice where a change materially affects your rights. For any question about your data, contact support@analyzemycar.com.
