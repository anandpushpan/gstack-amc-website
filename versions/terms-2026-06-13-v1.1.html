2. The service

AnalyzeMyCar provides vehicle information reports for UK cars, built from AI analysis and third-party data (OEM data, DVLA and DVSA MOT records). A report can include a vehicle overview and condition grade, MOT and mileage history, a digital service book, a factory build sheet, a grade-based market valuation, and an optional AI Visual Assessment. Reports are provided for information only and should not be the sole basis for a financial, legal or purchasing decision.

10. Visual Assessment and AI

The AI Visual Assessment estimates a vehicle's condition and likely repair costs from photographs you upload. It is indicative only, depends on the quality of the photos and third-party data, and does not constitute professional advice, a certified valuation or a repair guarantee.
