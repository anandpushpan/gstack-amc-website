Refund Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026 · Version 1.0 (draft)
⚠ Draft, pending legal review. These terms match what the site states today. The statutory cancellation wording marked [TO CONFIRM] needs legal review before launch. It is not legal advice.

This policy explains when and how you can get a refund on credits you have bought from AnalyzeMyCar.

Your first report is free

Your first report is free and includes every module except the AI Visual Assessment. There is no charge for it, so there is nothing to refund.

Refunds on credits

  • Unused credits can be refunded within 14 days of purchase.
  • Used credits are charged at £1 each and that amount is deducted from your refund.
  • VAT is non-refundable.
  • Refunds are processed within 5 to 10 business days, back to your original payment method.
  • Credits expire 180 days after purchase. Expired credits cannot be refunded.

A worked example

You buy a 40-credit pack and use 6 credits, then request a refund within 14 days. We refund the 34 unused credits, less VAT, with the 6 used credits charged at £1 each.

Your statutory rights

If you are a consumer, you may have a statutory right to cancel within 14 days under the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013. Because credits give access to digital content and reports are delivered immediately, that right can be affected once you start using credits. How to request a refund

Email info@analyzemycar.com from the address on your account, with your order details. We will confirm the refund amount before processing it.

Questions

For anything about refunds or billing, contact info@analyzemycar.com.
