1. Who we are

AnalyzeMyCar is operated by GATEWAY NINTEC UK LTD, a company registered in England and Wales, at 265-269 Kingston Road, London SW19 3NW. VAT number 385519364. AnalyzeMyCar is part of the AutoDAP B.V. group (Netherlands).

For any privacy question, email info@analyzemycar.com. We are the data controller for the personal data described here. Our ICO registration number is [TO CONFIRM].

2. What we collect

  • Account details: your name and email address when you create a free account.
  • Vehicle lookups: the registration or VIN you enter, and the reports you generate.
  • Payment details: when you buy credits, payment is processed by our payment provider [TO CONFIRM: e.g. Stripe]. We do not store your full card number.
  • Usage data: how you use the site (pages, actions, device and browser type, approximate location from IP) to run and improve the service.
  • Communications: messages you send us at our support address.

4. Vehicle data we look up

When you check a vehicle, we query official and manufacturer data sources about that vehicle, including DVLA vehicle data, DVSA MOT records, and OEM build and service data through the AutoDAP network. This is data about the vehicle, not about you. You are responsible for having a legitimate reason to look up a vehicle you do not own.

5. Who we share data with

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with:

  • Our payment processor, to take payment [TO CONFIRM].
  • Hosting and infrastructure providers that run the service [TO CONFIRM: e.g. Vercel and any database host].
  • Data providers we query on your behalf (DVLA, DVSA, OEM via AutoDAP).
  • Other group companies within AutoDAP B.V. where needed to run the service [TO CONFIRM].
  • Authorities, where we are legally required to.

6. How long we keep it

We keep account and report data for as long as your account is active, and afterwards only as long as needed for legal, accounting or fraud-prevention reasons. Specific retention periods: [TO CONFIRM].

7. International transfers

Because AnalyzeMyCar is part of a Netherlands-based group, some data may be processed inside the EU. Transfers between the UK and EU rely on the UK's adequacy decision for the EEA. Any transfer outside the UK or EEA is protected by appropriate safeguards such as the UK International Data Transfer Agreement. Details: [TO CONFIRM].

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access your data, correct it, ask us to erase it, restrict or object to processing, and request portability. Where we rely on consent, you can withdraw it at any time. To exercise any right, email info@analyzemycar.com. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies to run the site and keep you signed in, and (with your consent) analytics cookies to understand usage. A full list of the cookies we set, their purpose and lifespan: [TO CONFIRM].

10. Security

We use encrypted connections and access controls to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect personal data and to notify you and the ICO of a breach where the law requires.

11. Changes and contact

We may update this policy as the product changes. We will post the new version here with an updated date. For any question about your data, contact info@analyzemycar.com.
