FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is the first report really free?
Yes. Your first full report is free and includes everything except the AI Visual Assessment: vehicle overview, MOT and mileage, digital service book, grade-based valuation and the build sheet. No card needed to run it.
What does AnalyzeMyCar actually check?
Vehicle overview and A-C condition grade, full MOT and mileage history (with clocking flags), OEM digital service book, factory build sheet and spec, a grade-based market valuation with projections, and an AI visual condition assessment with itemised repair costs. Sourced from DVLA, DVSA and OEM data.
How do credits work?
After your free report, additional checks use credits: Vehicle overview (1), Grade-based valuation (2), Build sheet (2), MOT and mileage (3), Digital service book (3), AI Visual Assessment (4), or a Full Report (10). Buy a pack once and spend it across as many cars as you like.
Do credits expire?
Yes. Your credits expire 180 days after purchase, so be sure to use them before they expire.
Can I get a refund?
Yes. Unused credits can be refunded within 14 days; used credits are charged at £1 each. VAT is non-refundable, and refunds are processed within 5 to 10 business days.
Do you cover imported and European cars?
AnalyzeMyCar is built on AutoDAP's European OEM data network, so we surface factory build and service detail many UK-only checks miss. Coverage depends on the vehicle and its country of origin. Run a free report to see what's available for yours.
